PROFILE OF HORTI CONSULT

Horti Consult is a registered Business Entity with Registration No. BN699572004 that is registered at the Registrar-General's Department, Accra-Ghana.
The Principal Activity and nature of Business Hort Consult undertakes include the following:
1. Provision of Horticultural Services
2. Provision of Agro-inputs
3. Training of Horticultural Personnel
4. Acquisition and Installation of Greenhouses and Nethouses
Other activities Horti Consult undertakes include research in Agronomy and physiology of Horticultural crops (fruits and vegetable crops), climate change and its impact on phenology of field and horticultural crops, value chain analysis of fruit and vegetable crops, hydroponics and soiless culture (use of biochar) for vegetable and fruit crops production.
Horti Consult CEO in the past has served as a consultant to several clients including the FAO, Millennium Challenge Account (MCA), USAID/TIPCEE, GIZ/MOAP, Ministry of Food and Agriculture (MOFA) and assisted several Farmer Based Organizations (FBOs) to build capacity to understand farming as business, develop business plans and training in GLOBALGAP for horticultural crops. Horti Consult CEO has attended Internal Auditing Training course, by CMi Certification Africa (pty) Ltd.
